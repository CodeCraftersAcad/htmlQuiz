Privacy Policy

We are collecting data about our website visitors through Google Analytics. The data collected includes:

  • Number of users.
  • Session statistics.
  • Approximate geolocation.
  • Browser and device information.
  • Demographic information: such as age, gender, and interests
  • Geographic information: such as country, region, and city
  • Behavioral information: such as pages visited, duration of visit, and bounce rate
  • Technology information: such as browser type, operating system, and screen resolution
  • Acquisition information: such as referral source and keywords used to find the site

Google Analytics also uses first-party cookies and JavaScript code to collect this data. It also allows you to collect data from other sources like AdWords, or from your website's CRM systems. It's important to note that Google Analytics does not collect personal information such as name, email address, or phone number unless you explicitly configure it to do so. And, we have NOT configured it to do so.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by using the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.
